About Bijoy's Restaurant

A petite 26 seater restaurant in a cozy house setting offering eclectic, internationally inspired seasonal fare. Bijoy and Sujata are chefs with 30 years of experience in 5-star hotels and trained at the Culinary Institute of America in New York. Their passion for cooking for others and their love of meeting people, has culminated in this restaurant.
